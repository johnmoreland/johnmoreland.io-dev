VEX ROBOTICS

overview

role : team captain and mentor
team number : 7700R
timeline : 2009 - 2015

Starting in 7th grade, I participated in an annual robotics competition. VEX robotics was my first experience with hands-on engineering. I often credit VEX as the reason I decided to pursue engineering.

In my last year of competition, my team was lucky enough to establish a partnership with Rolling Robots, a local robotics workshop. We began mentoring their middle school teams. Rolling Robots now has 7 VEX teams.
